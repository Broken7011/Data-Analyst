What is Business Data Analyst? 
-> Understanding the role and impact in data-driven decision making 

> Key Responsibilities
Data collection and analysis
Interpreting and visualizing data trends
Communicating insights to stakeholders
Supporting business decisions with data
Creating dashboards and reports

> Core skils of business data analyst
Data analysis and visualization
Strong business acumen
Proficiency in tools: Excel, SQL, Power BI, Python
Effective communication and storytelling with data
Critical Thinking and attention to detail
